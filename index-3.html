🛡️ Prevention Science

Stay Ahead of Disease

Science-backed strategies that reduce disease risk and protect your crop yield season after season.

💧

Water at Ground Level

Always use drip irrigation or water at soil surface — never overhead. Wet foliage creates high humidity triggering most fungal diseases.

🔄

Rotate Your Crops

Alternate crop families every season. A 3-year rotation (cereal → legume → vegetable) dramatically reduces soil-borne pathogens.

✂️

Remove Infected Plants

At first sign of disease, remove and bag infected material immediately. Never compost — burn or bury to prevent spore spread.

📅

Scout Fields Weekly

Inspect leaves top and bottom every week. Early detection is the most powerful tool — treatment at first sign is 10× more effective.

🌬️

Improve Air Circulation

Maintain spacing and prune dense canopy. Good airflow dries leaves quickly — humidity above 80% for 6+ hours triggers fungal infection.

🌱

Use Resistant Varieties

Plant certified disease-resistant seed varieties. Check local agricultural extension services — resistance is your cheapest protection.

🌡️

Monitor Weather

High humidity + warm temperatures + 8+ hours of leaf wetness creates infection conditions for most pathogens. Act before rain events.

🧪

Maintain Soil Health

Keep balanced soil pH (6.0–7.0), adequate potassium and calcium. Nutritionally stressed plants are far more susceptible to all diseases.
